## App Description

Brand of a Champion is the platform empowering high school football athletes to reach the next level — through professional coaching, recruiting exposure, and personal development.

Our nonprofit has helped athletes earn scholarships to top D1 programs including Texas A&M, UCLA, Tennessee, Florida State, Utah, and Indiana. Now you can access the same system from your phone.

WHAT YOU GET:

• RECRUITING DATABASE — Browse NCAA Division I FBS football programs, coaching staffs, and contact information. Find the right school for your athlete.

• ATHLETE PROFILES — See our current and alumni athletes, their stats, and where they've committed. Real results from a real program.

• BEHIND THE BRAND — Watch our professional documentary series following high school football programs through their entire seasons. Eight full episodes of the Douglas County Tigers' 2025 playoff run.

• SCHOLARSHIP SEARCH — Search thousands of scholarships tailored for student-athletes. Filter by grade, state, and category.

• FOOTBALL TRAINING — Book sessions with professional position coaches for running backs, quarterbacks, wide receivers, linemen, linebackers, and defensive backs.

• GEORGIA MEDIA — Your source for Georgia high school football news, recruiting updates, and daily polls.

• COMMUNITY — Connect with coaches, athletes, and families in the Brand of a Champion network.

• APPAREL — Shop the BOAC collection. Every purchase supports our mission.

SUBSCRIPTION PLANS:
• Basic: $4.99/month — Coaching and platform access
• Premium: $14.99/month — Enhanced coaching, exposure, and training
• Recruiting: $19.99/month — Full recruiting services with college coach exposure

Brand of a Champion is a registered nonprofit. We believe every athlete deserves access to development regardless of their family's financial situation.

Pride. Power. Perseverance. Beyond the Game.
